About the foundation

Open-source.
No compromise.

Sparx.Foundation is an open-source security engineering organization building tooling for cybersecurity research, systems programming, and low-level infrastructure.

We operate at the intersection of kernel engineering, cryptography, and offensive security research. Our projects — from C2 infrastructure to Rust core libraries — are built in the open, licensed transparently under our FOUL (Fair Open-Use License), and engineered to the highest technical standard.

  • ◆ Open-source first — transparent, auditable, reproducible
  • ◆ Rust & C++ — performance and correctness at every layer
  • ◆ Research-grade security, production-grade engineering
